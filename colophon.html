What is this project?


This project examines the different aspects that feed into the stigma around black cats.

Lickorice Loaf was chosen as a playful twist on the website's name, reminiscent of black cats' playful nature — 'Lickorice' reflects their sleek, dark fur. And, well have you seen how they sit? Loaf refers to when they sit, hiding their paws and tails, making them look like cute little loaves of bread.

This website navigates the history of that stigma by presenting the various cultural folklore stories that have contributed to the current perception.
By shedding light on the past, the current perception starts to make sense. Readers are encouraged to reflect on how their perception has changed after reading through the pages of this website.
Ultimetly, This project aims to explore the historical origins of the stigma associated with black cats.

*This entire website was set in pf-videotext by Panos Vassiliou from Parachute Typefoundry.
